I am an Economist at the Federal Trade Commission and a Senior Advisor to the Chief Economist in the Office of the Under Secretary at the US Department of Education. I study the connection between education and the labor market. I'm especially interested in community colleges, career-technical education, and postsecondary education policy.

Any opinions on this website are mine alone and do not represent those of the Department of Education, the Federal Trade Commission or its Commissioners.
